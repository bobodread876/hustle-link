How it works

From chat to sats in three steps

Your customers already order in the chat. Hustle Link just closes the money loop.

01

Customer orders like always

"3 box food, $800 each, drop off at Papine." Plain text, patois, shorthand — the AI agent reads it and structures the order.

02

Invoice lands in the chat

The total converts to sats at the live rate and a Lightning invoice appears in the thread — payable from any Lightning wallet, anywhere on Earth.

03

You get paid. Instantly.

Sats settle straight to your Flash wallet with the order in the memo. No chargebacks, no waiting on cash, no bank account needed.

Why Hustle Link

Built for the hustle, not the boardroom

🔓

Zero custody, zero credentials

Invoices are generated through your existing Flash lightning address. Nobody — including us — ever touches your money.

📱

No new app for vendors

If you can run your business on WhatsApp, you can use Hustle Link. The chat is the point of sale.

🌍

Sell to the whole world

Lightning invoices are payable from any wallet in any country. Diaspora customers can pay for Mummy's Sunday dinner from Brooklyn.

🧾

Orders, not just payments

Items, delivery spot, buyer name and number — structured from the message and attached to the payment memo. Close off the day with one command.
